Precision Weather and News

Purpose

The purpose of Precision-Weather is to provide users with up-to-date weather, and news related to the region. Allowing them to plan their activities and stay safe during storms. The goal is to be able to allow users who are either on the go or planning events to be able to view weather specific data comfortably, regardless of the device they are using.
