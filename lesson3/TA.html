Precision Weather and News

Assignment Audience page

Target Audience

Target audience are the residents and visitors of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. Mainly those who are looking for up-to-date information regarding events and forecasted weather.

Personas

  1. Jane Doe: Age 34. Mother of three. Looking for events to get her kids out of the house. Has her day well planned around nap time and school. Uses the website from her laptop when planning out her week. Uses the website on the go from her phone to get up to date weather information to make sure kids are dressed appropriatly.

  2. Lavell Edwards: 90 year-old Retired football coach who really enjoys geyser park and the lava hot springs. He is also an avid fisher in Fish Haven. Lavell lives in Orem but frequently makes the trip to Soda Springs. Lavell primarily uses the website on his Large tablet that his players got him as a retirement gift. He uses the site for weather information to make sure the trips are safe.

Scenarios

  1. Jane Doe heard from a friend that the Preston splash pad was going to be putting on an event sometime towards the end of the week. Jane wants to know more so she goes on the website to find the date of the event with details along with the weather forecast for that day to make sure it is warm enough to play in the splash pad.

  2. The 4th of July is coming up Jane wants to know if there will be a fireworks show near her. She then goes on the website to check upcoming events and plans accordingly.

  3. Lavell wants to head to Fish Haven to celebrate after watching BYU win in Logan. He wants to make sure the weather is ok for him to drive up there and go fishing

  4. Jane is the maid of honor for her friends wedding. Her friend wants to have an outdoor wedding. She will use the website to plan some of the details. Ensuring that no events are taking place and that no rain is in the forecast.

  5. Lavell is planning his annual trip to Geyser Park and wants to make sure every detail is perfect, rain or shine, but being almost 91 wants to make sure that he dresses warm enough.
